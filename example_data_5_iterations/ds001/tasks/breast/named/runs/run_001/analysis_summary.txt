Analysis summary - ds001_breast (n=50,000)
===========================================

Outcome: pfs_months (mean 4.69, SD 2.50, range 0-16.7).

Across 10 iterations I tested main effects, biomarker-treatment interactions, multivariable predictors, and demographic/SES disparities. The dataset's signal structure is dominated by a sharply-restricted treatment effect (palbociclib in an ER+/HER2-/PIK3CA-wt stratum) and a small set of robust prognostic factors (ECOG PS, stage IV, brain metastases, albumin, weight loss, age, Ki67, LDH).

1. Prognostic main effects (clinical/laboratory)
------------------------------------------------
- ECOG PS: -1.18 mo per unit (p ~ 0). One of the strongest signals in the data.
- Stage IV: -1.54 mo (p ~ 0).
- Brain metastases: -1.05 mo (p = 1.5e-178).
- Albumin: +0.50 mo per g/dL (p = 9e-112).
- 6-month weight loss: -0.075 mo per % (p = 4e-148).
- Ki67%: -0.019 mo per % (p = 3e-93).
- LDH: -0.0005 mo per U/L (p = 2e-4).
- Calcium: -0.064 mo per mg/dL (p = 0.004).
- Alkaline phosphatase: -7e-4 mo per U/L (p = 0.003), tiny.
- Age: +0.176 mo per year (p ~ 0). Surprising direction; older patients have longer PFS in this cohort. The age x stage_iv interaction is negative (-0.011 per year per stage IV, p = 3e-12), so the protective association attenuates in advanced disease.
- Symptom grades (fatigue, pain, dyspnea, cough, appetite loss): no significant main effects.

2. Biomarker main effects
-------------------------
- PIK3CA mutation: -0.56 mo overall (p = 6e-144). Concentrated entirely in ER+ patients (-0.80 mo in ER+ vs ~0 mo in ER-, p_ER+ = 2e-192).
- HER2-low: +0.11 mo (p = 1e-6); persists at +0.13 mo in multivariable adjustment (p = 6e-47).
- TP53, BRCA1, BRCA2 (alone), her2_amplification, MET exon 14, RET/ROS1/NTRK/NRG1 fusions, BRAF V600E, FGFR alterations, CDKN2A loss, KEAP1, PTEN loss, MSI-high: no detectable PFS main effects, with the partial exception of MET exon 14 skipping (-0.14 mo, p = 0.024) which would not survive multiple-testing correction.

3. Treatment effects (overall, unstratified)
--------------------------------------------
- Palbociclib: +1.10 mo (p ~ 0). The only therapy with a strong unconditional benefit.
- Trastuzumab: -0.06 mo (p = 0.029) - directionally NEGATIVE overall.
- Pembrolizumab: -0.07 mo (p = 0.018) - directionally negative overall.
- Tamoxifen: +0.04 mo (p = 0.08), trend.
- Olaparib: -0.04 mo (p = 0.33).
- Sacituzumab govitecan: -0.01 mo (p = 0.85).

4. Treatment x biomarker interactions
-------------------------------------
The headline finding is the palbociclib x ER x PIK3CA structure:
- palbociclib x er_positive interaction: +1.59 mo (p = 9e-229).
- Stratified within ER+: palbo effect +1.58 mo; within ER-: ~0.
- Three-way palbo x er x PIK3CA-mutation interaction: -2.43 mo (p = 2e-128). PIK3CA mutation completely abolishes the palbociclib benefit within ER+.
- Stratified across the 2x2 ER x PIK3CA grid: palbo effect is +2.38 mo only in ER+/PIK3CA-wt; ~0 in the other three cells.
- Drilling further: within ER+/PIK3CA-wt, palbo effect is +2.91 mo when HER2- but ~0 when HER2+. So the palbociclib responder group is essentially ER+/HER2-/PIK3CA-wild-type.
- HER2-low sub-modifier: within ER+, palbo effect is somewhat larger when HER2-low (+1.94 vs +1.36 mo, interaction p = 1e-23).

Other targeted-therapy interactions:
- Olaparib x BRCA1/2 (combined): interaction +0.40 mo (p = 0.021); subgroup PFS difference among BRCA1/2 carriers +0.35 mo (p = 0.041); among non-carriers -0.06 mo. A modest but real PARP-inhibitor benefit confined to germline BRCA carriers.
- Trastuzumab x HER2+: not detected (interaction p = 0.69; subgroup effect in HER2+ is -0.08 mo, p = 0.18). The classic anti-HER2 signal is absent in this cohort.
- Pembrolizumab x MSI-high: not detected (interaction p = 0.47; subgroup effect +0.14 mo in MSI-high, p = 0.64; n only 86 treated).
- Pembrolizumab x triple-negative: subgroup effect +0.07 mo (p = 0.22), directionally consistent with biology but not statistically supported.
- Sacituzumab x triple-negative or HER2-low: not detected.
- Tamoxifen x ER+ or x premenopausal ER+: not detected (largest stratum diff +0.11 mo, p = 0.07).

5. Demographic / SES disparities
--------------------------------
- Race/ethnicity (white, black, hispanic, asian, other): one-way ANOVA F = 0.85, p = 0.49. Cell means within 0.08 mo of each other. No detectable disparity.
- Insurance type (medicare, medicaid, private, uninsured): ANOVA F = 0.31, p = 0.82.
- Rural residence: +0.027 mo (p = 0.34).
- Education years: -0.003 mo per year (p = 0.39).
- Sex: female vs male diff -0.013 mo (p = 0.56). Note that sex_female=1 in 87% of the cohort; the remaining 13% male patients with breast cancer showed no PFS difference.
- BMI, smoking pack-years: no significant main effects.

6. SNP scan
-----------
Across 24 candidate SNPs, the smallest unadjusted p-value was 0.026 (snp_rs1801131, -0.054 mo). After Bonferroni correction (alpha = 0.002), no SNP was significant. Germline genotypes in this panel are not informative for PFS in this cohort.

7. Comorbidities and metastatic-site features
---------------------------------------------
None of: diabetes, hypertension, COPD, CKD, heart failure, CAD, atrial fibrillation, VTE history, autoimmune disease, hepatitis B/C, HIV, prior malignancy, depression/anxiety, ILD, pleural/pericardial effusion, adrenal mets, contralateral lung mets - reached significance.
- Liver mets and bone mets did NOT show negative PFS main effects despite biological expectation (diff +0.018 and +0.038 mo, p > 0.17).
- Prior surgery: +0.066 mo (p = 0.005). Prior chemotherapy/radiation/immunotherapy/targeted therapy: no signals.

8. Multivariable model (R^2 = 0.844)
------------------------------------
With ECOG, stage IV, brain mets, age, albumin, 6-month weight loss, Ki67, LDH, HER2-low, ER, PIK3CA, palbociclib + interactions, and olaparib*BRCA included:
- Three-way palbo*er*(1 - pik3ca_mutation) coefficient: +2.39 mo (p ~ 0). Captures essentially all of the palbociclib benefit.
- Bare palbociclib (+0.003), palbo*er (-0.0003), olap*BRCA (-0.013) all non-significant once the three-way term is in the model.
- HER2-low independently +0.13 mo (p = 6e-47).
- Tumor/host factors retain expected directions and magnitudes.

9. Hypotheses supported vs refuted
----------------------------------
Strongly supported:
- Palbociclib benefits PFS (overall and within ER+/HER2-/PIK3CA-wt).
- Palbociclib benefit is restricted to ER+ (positive palbo*ER interaction) and further to PIK3CA-wt and HER2- within ER+.
- ECOG PS, stage IV, brain mets, low albumin, weight loss, high Ki67, high LDH, hypercalcemia all worsen PFS.
- PIK3CA mutation worsens PFS specifically within ER+ patients.
- HER2-low confers a small positive PFS advantage independent of treatment.
- Olaparib benefit is restricted to BRCA1/2 carriers (modest interaction).

Refuted in this dataset (despite clinical expectation):
- Trastuzumab x HER2+ benefit (no detectable interaction).
- Pembrolizumab x MSI-high or triple-negative benefit (no detectable signal).
- Sacituzumab govitecan x triple-negative or HER2-low benefit.
- Tamoxifen x ER+ benefit.
- Liver mets and bone mets as PFS-shortening factors.
- Race/ethnicity and insurance disparities in PFS.
- TP53, MSI-high, and other oncogenic alterations beyond PIK3CA and HER2-low as prognostic.
- Germline SNP main effects (after multiple-testing correction).

10. Bottom line
---------------
PFS variability in this 50,000-patient breast cohort is best explained by a small set of clinical/laboratory prognostics (ECOG, stage IV, brain mets, age, albumin, weight loss, Ki67, LDH) plus a single dominant treatment-biomarker structure: palbociclib produces a ~2.9-month PFS gain, but only in ER+/HER2-/PIK3CA-wt patients. PIK3CA mutation is harmful chiefly because it abolishes this palbociclib responsiveness in ER+ disease. A modest secondary signal exists for olaparib among BRCA1/2 carriers. Other targeted/immune therapies (trastuzumab, pembrolizumab, sacituzumab govitecan, tamoxifen) and the candidate-SNP panel showed no detectable PFS effects in this cohort. No race/ethnicity or insurance-type disparity in PFS was observed.
