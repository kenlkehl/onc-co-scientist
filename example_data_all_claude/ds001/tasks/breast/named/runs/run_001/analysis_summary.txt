ds001_breast — Analysis Summary
================================

Cohort: 50,000 patients with one outcome variable, pfs_months (mean 4.69, SD 2.50).
Approach: 25 iterations of propose / test / refine. Tests were OLS regressions of pfs_months
on each predictor, adjusted in multivariable models for age_years, ecog_ps, stage_iv,
has_brain_mets, albumin_g_dl, weight_loss_pct_6mo, ki67_pct, er_positive, her2_positive,
pik3ca_mutation, brca1/brca2 status, and the other treatment indicators where applicable.
A multivariable model with all 37 features achieved R^2 = 0.79.

1. Strong main-effect prognostic factors (all p < 1e-200 in the multivariable model)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Direction confirmed for the following predictors of pfs_months:
  - age_years              +0.176 months per year (older patients have longer PFS in this
                              cohort; the relationship is convex with age^2 also positive)
  - albumin_g_dl           +0.476 per g/dL
  - er_positive            +0.532
  - treatment_palbociclib  +1.101 (main effect; see heterogeneity section below)
  - ecog_ps                -1.171 per unit
  - stage_iv               -1.549
  - has_brain_mets         -0.983
  - her2_positive          -0.471
  - pik3ca_mutation        -0.582
  - ki67_pct               -0.019 per percentage point
  - weight_loss_pct_6mo    -0.077 per percent
  - ldh_u_l                -0.00052 per U/L (small but significant, p=2e-16)

2. Hypotheses NOT supported / refuted
-------------------------------------
  - tumor_size_cm has no detectable association with pfs_months (p=0.65).
  - sex_female (p=0.21), pr_positive after adjusting for er_positive (p=0.32), her2_low
    (p=0.86), postmenopausal (p=0.42), node_positive (p=0.08), brca2_mutation (p=0.20),
    crp_mg_l (p=0.86), nlr (p=0.56), and all ten of the liver-function/electrolyte labs
    (alkaline phosphatase, AST, ALT, total bilirubin, creatinine, BUN, sodium, potassium,
    calcium, hemoglobin) lacked independent association after adjustment.
  - brca1_mutation showed a small adjusted negative association (-0.068, p=0.036), but
    this is small and not robust to multiple testing.

3. Treatment main effects
-------------------------
Only treatment_palbociclib showed a strong adjusted main effect (+1.10 months, p<<0.001).
All other treatments — tamoxifen, trastuzumab, olaparib, sacituzumab_govitecan,
pembrolizumab — had adjusted main-effect coefficients between -0.03 and +0.03 with
p-values from 0.05 to 0.48; none clinically meaningful.

4. Treatment-effect heterogeneity searches
------------------------------------------
A systematic screen of 6 treatments x 12 binary biomarker / clinical modifiers
(72 interactions) plus 6 treatments x 10 continuous modifiers (60 interactions) was
performed. Findings, by treatment:

(a) treatment_palbociclib — large, well-defined gating subgroup
   - palbociclib x er_positive  interaction  +1.56  (p<1e-300)
   - palbociclib x her2_positive interaction -1.32  (p<1e-300)
   - palbociclib x pik3ca_mutation interaction -1.66 (p<1e-300)
   - palbociclib x ki67_pct  interaction       -0.053 per % (p<1e-300)
   In the joint model (palbociclib interacted with all four modifiers simultaneously)
   the implied effect is approximately:
       palbociclib effect on pfs_months
         ≈ +1.665 + 1.531·er_positive − 1.324·her2_positive
                − 1.643·pik3ca_mutation − 0.053·ki67_pct
   So in ER+/HER2-/PIK3CA-WT/ki67=10 the model predicts ~+2.7 months; in any HER2+ or
   PIK3CA+ patient the effect collapses to near zero regardless of the other modifiers.

   In stratified subgroup analyses:
     ER+/HER2-/PIK3CA-WT (n=18,681)              adj +2.92, p<<0.001
     ER+/HER2-/PIK3CA-WT AND ki67<20 (n=14,174)   adj +3.85, p<<0.001
     ER+/HER2-/PIK3CA-WT AND ki67>=20 (n=4,507)   adj -0.006, p=0.32
     ER-/HER2-/PIK3CA-WT/ki67<20  (n=6,081)        adj -0.005, p=0.36
     ER+/HER2+/PIK3CA-WT/ki67<20  (n=3,147)        adj +0.007, p=0.41
     ER+/HER2-/PIK3CA-mut/ki67<20 (n=7,513)        adj +0.004, p=0.35
   Each of er_positive=1, her2_positive=0, pik3ca_mutation=0, and ki67_pct<20 is
   individually necessary: dropping any one collapses the adjusted effect to ~0.

   The benefit is similar across stage_iv strata (within ER+/HER2-/PIK3CA-WT/ki67<20:
   +3.86 months in stage_iv=0, +3.79 in stage_iv=1).

(b) treatment_sacituzumab_govitecan — small but specific brain-mets benefit
   - sacituzumab_govitecan x has_brain_mets interaction +0.195 (p=0.002)
     Within has_brain_mets=1 (n=5,090): adj +0.205 months, p=0.0005.
     Within has_brain_mets=1 AND stage_iv=1 (n=1,528): adj +0.350, p=0.0006.
   - In has_brain_mets=0 the effect is null (adj +0.012, p=0.50).
   - The HER2-low x ER+ stratum showed a borderline signal (adj +0.10, p=0.035) but it
     does not survive the same robustness checks.

(c) treatment_trastuzumab — no detectable benefit anywhere
   Even within the canonical her2_positive=1 subgroup (n=9,060): adj coef -0.0003, p=0.95.
   No other subgroup is positive.

(d) treatment_olaparib — no detectable benefit anywhere
   Within BRCA1- or BRCA2-mut carriers (n=2,499): adj coef +0.024, p=0.76. The
   uncorrected mean PFS is +0.35 higher in BRCA carriers given olaparib, but this
   reflects confounding (older / better-baseline carriers); after adjustment, no signal.

(e) treatment_pembrolizumab — no clinically meaningful benefit
   The only nominally significant interaction was pembrolizumab x er_positive (-0.074,
   p=0.029) — a tiny negative-in-ER+ signal that does not translate into a positive
   subgroup elsewhere. In TNBC (ER-/PR-/HER2-, n=10,496): adj coef +0.008, p=0.16.

(f) treatment_tamoxifen — no detectable benefit, including in the canonical ER+ subgroup
   ER+ (n=34,886): adj coef +0.001, p=0.93. Pre/post-menopausal stratification adds
   no signal.

5. Final best-supported subgroup hypotheses (one per treatment with usable variation)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - treatment_palbociclib improves pfs_months by approximately +3.8 months in patients
    with er_positive=1 AND her2_positive=0 AND pik3ca_mutation=0 AND ki67_pct<20
    (n=14,174 patients, ~28% of the cohort). All four predicates are individually
    necessary; outside this subgroup the effect is null.
  - treatment_sacituzumab_govitecan improves pfs_months modestly in patients with
    has_brain_mets=1 (~+0.2 months overall, ~+0.35 if also stage_iv=1); the effect
    is null in has_brain_mets=0.
  - treatment_trastuzumab, treatment_olaparib, treatment_pembrolizumab, and
    treatment_tamoxifen show no PFS benefit anywhere in this dataset, including
    their canonical biomarker subgroups.

6. Caveats and observations
---------------------------
  - The strong positive age_years effect (older = longer PFS) and the lack of any
    benefit for trastuzumab / olaparib / pembrolizumab / tamoxifen even in their
    canonical biomarker subgroups are unusual relative to clinical-trial expectations
    in real-world breast cancer. They likely reflect properties of how this synthetic
    or vendor-aggregated cohort was constructed rather than ground-truth biology.
  - Only 44.7% of records have sex_female=1 in this "breast" cohort, but sex itself
    has no detectable association with pfs_months after adjustment.
  - All stated treatment effects come from cross-sectional, observational adjustment
    rather than randomization, so confounding by indication cannot be excluded; the
    palbociclib subgroup signal is, however, large, biologically coherent, and robust
    to dropping any single predicate of the four-element subgroup definition.
