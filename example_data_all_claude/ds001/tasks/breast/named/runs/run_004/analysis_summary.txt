Analysis summary — ds001_breast (n=50,000; outcome: pfs_months)
================================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
Single time-to-progression-style outcome (pfs_months; mean 4.69, median 4.54,
range 0–~17). I performed 15 iterations of propose-test-refine: marginal
treatment effects -> marginal feature effects -> biomarker effects -> mechanistic
treatment-by-biomarker interactions -> exhaustive interaction screen -> refined
multi-predicate subgroup discovery for the only treatment that showed
heterogeneity. The cohort labeling does not appear to be sex-restricted
(sex_female prevalence is 0.45) but biomarker prevalences (ER+ 70%, HER2+ 18%,
HER2-low 37%, BRCA1 2.6%, BRCA2 2.4%, PIK3CA 35%, TNBC 21%) are consistent with
breast-cancer-vendor data.

PROGNOSTIC FEATURES (main effects)
----------------------------------
After full multivariable adjustment (R^2 = 0.79):

Strong negative prognostic factors (shorter PFS):
  - stage_iv:                   -1.55 mo (p<<1e-300)
  - ecog_ps (per step):         -1.17 mo (p<<1e-300)
  - has_brain_mets:             -0.98 mo (p<<1e-300)
  - pik3ca_mutation:            -0.58 mo (p<<1e-300)
  - her2_positive:              -0.47 mo (p<<1e-300)
  - weight_loss_pct_6mo (per %): -0.077 mo (p<<1e-300)
  - ki67_pct (per %):           -0.019 mo (p<<1e-300)

Strong positive prognostic factors (longer PFS):
  - er_positive:                +0.54 mo (p<<1e-300)
  - albumin_g_dl (per g/dL):    +0.48 mo (p<<1e-300)
  - age_years (per year):       +0.176 mo (p<<1e-300)  *direction inverted*

Comments on the age finding (h2.4 / h13.1): older patients had substantially
LONGER PFS in this cohort (Pearson r = +0.70 between age and pfs_months;
mean PFS rises monotonically from 1.5 mo at 40-50y to 8.2 mo at 80+y). This
direction is opposite to the typical clinical expectation. The signal is robust
to adjustment for ECOG, stage, brain mets, and all biomarkers, so it is a
structural property of the dataset rather than residual confounding.

Null marginal predictors: tumor_size_cm, crp_mg_l, nlr, hemoglobin_g_dl,
sex_female, node_positive, postmenopausal, brca1_mutation, brca2_mutation,
and most laboratory chemistries (AST, ALT, bilirubin, creatinine, BUN, sodium,
potassium, calcium, alkaline phosphatase). HER2-low has only a small marginal
+0.11 mo effect that does not survive adjustment.

TREATMENT MAIN EFFECTS
----------------------
Marginal pfs_months difference (treated minus control):

  treatment_palbociclib:           +1.10 mo (p<<1e-300)   <-- only true effect
  treatment_tamoxifen:             +0.04 mo (p=0.08)      null
  treatment_trastuzumab:           -0.06 mo (p=0.03)      negligible/likely confounded
  treatment_olaparib:              -0.04 mo (p=0.33)      null
  treatment_sacituzumab_govitecan: -0.01 mo (p=0.85)      null
  treatment_pembrolizumab:         -0.07 mo (p=0.02)      negligible/likely confounded

After full multivariable adjustment, only palbociclib retains a meaningful
positive effect (+1.10 mo, p<<1e-300). All other five treatments have adjusted
coefficients with |effect| < 0.04 mo and p > 0.05.

CLINICALLY MOTIVATED SUBGROUP CHECKS — five "negative" treatments
-----------------------------------------------------------------
For each treatment I tested its expected biomarker-defined subgroup
hypothesis explicitly (interaction model + within-subgroup adjusted
regression):

- Tamoxifen × er_positive interaction: -0.06 (p=0.27); ER+ adjusted tamoxifen
  coef +0.003 (p=0.87). REFUTED — no preferential effect in ER-positive
  patients.

- Trastuzumab × her2_positive interaction: -0.03 (p=0.69); HER2+ adjusted
  trastuzumab coef -0.0004 (p=0.95). REFUTED — no preferential effect in
  HER2-positive patients.

- Olaparib in BRCA1- or BRCA2-mutated subgroup: marginal +0.35 mo (p=0.04),
  but adjusted coefficient +0.025 (p=0.75). The marginal signal was driven
  by case-mix, not a real treatment effect. REFUTED in adjusted analysis.

- Sacituzumab-govitecan in TNBC, HER2-low, and HR+/HER2- subgroups: all
  null (|effect| < 0.1 mo, p > 0.2 throughout, adjusted p > 0.3). REFUTED.

- Pembrolizumab in TNBC: marginal +0.07 mo (p=0.22); adjusted +0.008
  (p=0.16). REFUTED.

So tamoxifen, trastuzumab, olaparib, sacituzumab-govitecan, and pembrolizumab
have no biomarker-defined PFS effect in this cohort.

PALBOCICLIB — TREATMENT-EFFECT HETEROGENEITY (the dataset's main story)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
An exhaustive treatment × feature interaction screen across all six
treatments and all 31 candidate modifiers showed that the six largest
heterogeneity signals all involve palbociclib:

  palbociclib × pik3ca_mutation: -1.62  p = 4e-255  (mutation suppresses)
  palbociclib × er_positive:     +1.59  p = 9e-229  (ER+ enables)
  palbociclib × ki67_pct (per %): -0.052 p = 1e-174 (high ki67 attenuates)
  palbociclib × her2_positive:   -1.36  p = 2e-118  (HER2+ suppresses)
  palbociclib × pr_positive:     +1.09  p = 3e-117  (largely correlated with ER+)
  palbociclib × her2_low:        +0.42  p = 9e-19   (mild enhancement)

After these, no other treatment-by-feature interaction reaches |coef|>0.5
or p<1e-10.

Subgroup-stratified palbociclib effect (mean pfs_months, palbo vs ctrl):

  ER+/HER2-/PIK3CA-WT (n=18,681 = 37.4%):   7.21 vs 4.30,  diff +2.91 (p<<1e-300)
  Outside ER+/HER2-/PIK3CA-WT (n=31,319):   4.32 vs 4.32,  diff +0.00 (p=0.91)

Within ER+/HER2-/PIK3CA-WT, the effect is further modified by ki67:

  Quartile 1 (ki67  1.0–7.5%):   9.23 vs 4.30,  diff +4.93 (p<<1e-300)
  Quartile 2 (ki67  7.6–12.2%):  9.24 vs 4.27,  diff +4.97 (p<<1e-300)
  Quartile 3 (ki67 12.3–19.6%):  6.00 vs 4.28,  diff +1.72 (p=1e-83)
  Quartile 4 (ki67 19.7–100%):   4.30 vs 4.35,  diff -0.05 (p=0.44)

That is, high proliferation (ki67 ≥ ~20%) abolishes palbociclib benefit even
within the ER+/HER2-/PIK3CA-WT subgroup. This pattern is mechanistically
plausible: rapidly proliferating tumors and PI3K-pathway-activated tumors are
both classic resistance contexts for CDK4/6 inhibition.

REFINED FAVORABLE SUBGROUP — final hypothesis
---------------------------------------------
The complete subgroup definition for palbociclib treatment effect on
pfs_months is:

   er_positive = 1  AND  her2_positive = 0  AND  pik3ca_mutation = 0  AND  ki67_pct < 20

In this refined subgroup (n=14,174 = 28.3% of cohort):
  - Unadjusted palbociclib effect:  +3.85 mo (8.13 vs 4.29, p<<1e-300)
  - Multivariable-adjusted effect:  +3.84 mo (p<<1e-300)

Outside this refined subgroup (n=35,826 = 71.7%):
  - Palbociclib effect: -0.002 mo (p=0.94)

Each of the four predicates is materially required — removing any one
predicate causes a substantial drop in the within-subgroup palbociclib
effect, and removing er_positive=1 collapses it to zero. PIK3CA mutation
status in particular acts as a clear suppressor: ER+/HER2-/PIK3CA-mut
patients receive no benefit from palbociclib (+0.02 mo, p=0.62), while
ER+/HER2-/PIK3CA-WT patients get +2.91 mo unadjusted.

CONCLUSIONS
-----------
1. Palbociclib is the only treatment in this dataset with detectable
   PFS effect, and its benefit is sharply heterogeneous: confined to
   ER-positive, HER2-non-amplified, PIK3CA-wildtype, low-ki67 (<20%) tumors.

2. None of tamoxifen, trastuzumab, olaparib, sacituzumab-govitecan, or
   pembrolizumab shows any meaningful PFS benefit in any tested
   biomarker-defined or laboratory-defined subgroup; their adjusted
   effects are all within ±0.04 months of zero. This includes tests in
   their canonical clinical subgroups (ER+ for tamoxifen; HER2+ for
   trastuzumab; BRCA1/2 mutated for olaparib; TNBC and HER2-low for
   sacituzumab-govitecan; TNBC for pembrolizumab).

3. Independent prognostic factors are dominated by stage IV, ECOG,
   brain mets, ER, HER2, PIK3CA, ki67, weight loss, albumin, and age.
   The age effect is positive (older = longer PFS), opposite to the
   typical clinical expectation; this is a robust structural feature
   of the dataset.

4. The single best-supported treatment-effect subgroup hypothesis for
   pfs_months in this cohort is:

      treatment_palbociclib increases pfs_months by approximately
      +3.8 months when er_positive=1 AND her2_positive=0 AND
      pik3ca_mutation=0 AND ki67_pct<20, with negligible effect
      otherwise.

   No analogous subgroup hypothesis is supported for the other five
   treatments.
