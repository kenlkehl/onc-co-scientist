Analysis Summary — ds001_breast (n = 50,000)
=============================================
Outcome: pfs_months (mean 4.69, SD 2.50, range 0 - 16.7)

Across 25 iterations I probed (a) treatment main effects, (b) biomarker main
effects, (c) disease-severity / prognostic markers, (d) labs and inflammation,
(e) treatment-by-biomarker interactions for the canonical "matched"
indications, (f) demographics / disparities, (g) comorbidities and symptoms,
(h) SNP screen, (i) treatment-treatment combinations, (j) refined / adjusted
multivariable models, and (k) sanity-check off-target markers.

----------------------------------------------------------------
1. TREATMENT MAIN EFFECTS (iter 1, 11)
----------------------------------------------------------------
- treatment_palbociclib: +1.10 months (unadjusted, p ~ 0). The single largest
  positive treatment signal in the cohort. The adjusted multivariable
  coefficient is essentially identical (+1.10, p ~ 0), so this is not a
  prognostic-confounding artifact.
- treatment_tamoxifen: +0.04 months (p = 0.08, NS).
- treatment_trastuzumab: -0.06 months unadjusted (p = 0.029); adjusted -0.01
  (p = 0.45). Slight negative crude difference is consistent with treatment
  channeling toward HER2+ patients, who have intrinsically worse prognosis.
- treatment_olaparib: -0.04 months (NS) overall.
- treatment_sacituzumab_govitecan: -0.01 months (NS) overall.
- treatment_pembrolizumab: -0.07 months unadjusted (p = 0.018); adjusted
  -0.03 (p = 0.055). Again, channeling toward harder-to-treat disease.

----------------------------------------------------------------
2. BIOMARKER / SUBTYPE MAIN EFFECTS (iter 2)
----------------------------------------------------------------
- er_positive: +0.57 months (p = 4e-143).  Strong favorable prognostic.
- pr_positive: +0.36 months (p = 6e-59).
- her2_positive: -0.46 months (p = 6e-70).  Adverse prognostic.
- her2_low: +0.11 months (p = 1e-6).
- pik3ca_mutation: -0.56 months (p = 6e-144).  Strong adverse signal,
  especially within ER+ (see iter 20).
- brca1_mutation, brca2_mutation, msi_high, tp53_mutation, pten_loss: all NS.

----------------------------------------------------------------
3. DISEASE-SEVERITY / PROGNOSTIC FACTORS (iter 3)
----------------------------------------------------------------
- stage_iv: -1.54 months (p ~ 0).  Largest single prognostic effect.
- has_brain_mets: -1.05 months (p = 1e-178).
- ecog_ps (continuous): -1.18 months per unit (p ~ 0).
- ki67_pct: -0.019 per percentage point (p = 3e-93).
- node_positive, liver_mets, bone_mets, tumor_size_cm: NS.
  (The latter is somewhat surprising clinically and points to a synthetic
  generator that did not encode tumor size into the survival signal.)

----------------------------------------------------------------
4. LAB / INFLAMMATION MARKERS (iter 4)
----------------------------------------------------------------
- albumin_g_dl: +0.50 months per g/dL (p = 9e-112). Strong favorable.
- weight_loss_pct_6mo: -0.075 months per percentage point (p = 4e-148).
- ldh_u_l: -5e-4 months per U/L (p = 2e-4), small but significant.
- alkaline_phosphatase_u_l: -7e-4 (p = 0.003).
- crp_mg_l, nlr, hemoglobin_g_dl: NS.

----------------------------------------------------------------
5. TREATMENT x BIOMARKER INTERACTIONS — the precision-medicine question
----------------------------------------------------------------
This was the central test of the cohort. Did the simulated EHR encode the
classical breast-cancer "matched-therapy" relationships?

(a) palbociclib x er_positive (iter 8, 23):
    Interaction coefficient +1.59, p ~ 0.  In ER+, palbociclib adds
    +1.58 months (p ~ 0); in ER-, no benefit.  The benefit survives
    adjustment for age, ECOG, stage_iv, brain mets, liver mets, LDH,
    albumin, CRP, NLR, and a poor-prognosis composite.  STRONGLY SUPPORTED.

(b) olaparib x brca_any (brca1 OR brca2; iter 7):
    Unadjusted interaction +0.40, p = 0.021.  Within BRCA-mutated
    patients (n = 2,499), olaparib adds +0.35 months (p = 0.041); in
    BRCA wild-type there is no benefit.  After adjustment for prognostic
    covariates and other interactions the BRCA*olaparib effect attenuates
    to +0.10, p = 0.32 (iter 23, 25), so the effect is real but small
    and partly confounded.  SUPPORTED but modest.

(c) trastuzumab x her2_positive (iter 6, 23):
    Interaction -0.03, p = 0.70.  Within HER2+, trastuzumab actually
    showed a -0.08 month non-significant trend (p = 0.15).  Within HER2-
    a non-significant -0.05 trend.  NOT SUPPORTED — the dataset does NOT
    encode the canonical trastuzumab-by-HER2 benefit.  This was the most
    striking negative finding clinically, and it appears robust.

(d) sacituzumab_govitecan x her2_low and x TNBC (iter 9):
    Both interactions NS.  Within TNBC (n=10,496), sacituzumab -0.085
    months (NS).  NOT SUPPORTED.

(e) pembrolizumab x msi_high (iter 10):
    Interaction +0.22, p = 0.47.  NS.
    pembrolizumab x TNBC: +0.18, p = 0.015 (marginal).  In TNBC,
    pembrolizumab is +0.07 months (p = 0.22) — direction-only.
    WEAK / EQUIVOCAL support.

(f) tamoxifen x er_positive (iter 5):
    Interaction -0.06, p = 0.27.  Within ER+ no significant tamoxifen
    benefit (+0.02, p = 0.45).  Within ER- a small +0.08 (p = 0.03).
    NOT SUPPORTED — tamoxifen efficacy is not selectively encoded for
    ER+ patients in this dataset.

----------------------------------------------------------------
6. REFINED INTERACTIONS (iter 17, 19, 20)
----------------------------------------------------------------
- palbociclib x tamoxifen within ER+: NS interaction (p = 0.78), so the
  combined regimen is additive rather than synergistic.
- palbociclib x weight_loss_pct_6mo within ER+: +0.015 (p = 0.04) —
  marginal; benefit slightly larger in cachectic patients.
- palbociclib x pik3ca_mutation within ER+: -2.34 (p ~ 0).  This is a
  large clinically-meaningful interaction: in ER+/PIK3CA-WT patients
  palbociclib delivers its full +~2-month benefit, but in ER+/PIK3CA-mut
  patients almost none of that benefit remains.  Strongly supported.
- trastuzumab x albumin in HER2+: NS.

----------------------------------------------------------------
7. OFF-LABEL / NEGATIVE-CONTROL CHECKS (iter 22)
----------------------------------------------------------------
- HER2- trastuzumab: -0.05 months (NS)
- BRCA-WT olaparib: -0.06 months (NS)
- ER- tamoxifen: +0.08 months (p = 0.03), surprisingly nominally
  significant but tiny.
The matched therapies are at least not actively harmful in unmatched
populations, but only palbociclib (in ER+) and olaparib (in BRCA+)
showed a real "match" benefit.

----------------------------------------------------------------
8. DEMOGRAPHICS / DISPARITIES (iter 12, 13)
----------------------------------------------------------------
- race_ethnicity ANOVA: p = 0.49.  No disparity signal.
- insurance_type (uninsured vs private): NS.
- rural_residence: NS.
- education_years: NS.
- sex_female: NS (and the cohort is ~45% male, which is biologically
  unusual for breast cancer — likely an artifact of the schema).
- age_years: positive slope of +0.176 months per year (p ~ 0).  This is
  the opposite direction of typical clinical intuition; in this synthetic
  dataset older patients have *longer* PFS. The likely explanation is
  channeling — younger patients in this cohort over-represent
  HER2-positive / TNBC / brain-met disease, all of which carry shorter
  PFS.
- postmenopausal: NS.

----------------------------------------------------------------
9. COMORBIDITIES AND SYMPTOMS (iter 14, 15)
----------------------------------------------------------------
None of diabetes, hypertension, COPD, CKD, heart failure, CAD,
autoimmune disease, prior malignancy, depression/anxiety, or
ILD-history showed any significant effect on pfs_months. Likewise
fatigue, pain, dyspnea, cough, and appetite-loss grades all NS.
The synthetic generator does NOT encode comorbidity burden into PFS.

----------------------------------------------------------------
10. SNP SCREEN (iter 16)
----------------------------------------------------------------
24 snp_* variables tested individually.  Only 1 nominal hit
(snp_rs1801131, slope -0.054, p = 0.026).  After Bonferroni
correction (alpha = 0.0021) zero SNPs survive.  No genotype-PFS signal.

11. PRIOR THERAPY / TIME (iter 18)
----------------------------------------------------------------
prior_lines_of_therapy, prior_immunotherapy, and years_since_diagnosis
all NS.  The dataset does not encode treatment-line attrition.

----------------------------------------------------------------
12. NEGATIVE-CONTROL BIOMARKERS (iter 24)
----------------------------------------------------------------
psa_ng_ml: p = 0.06; ros1_fusion: p = 0.90.  As expected, irrelevant
non-breast biomarkers are null.

----------------------------------------------------------------
13. COMPOSITE PROGNOSTIC INDICATOR (iter 25)
----------------------------------------------------------------
poor_prog = (ECOG>=2 OR brain_mets OR liver_mets OR albumin<3.5):
-0.92 months unadjusted, -0.89 months adjusted (p ~ 0). After adjusting
for poor_prog plus age and stage:
  trastuzumab x HER2  : +0.017  (p = 0.67)  — not supported
  olaparib  x BRCA_any: +0.10   (p = 0.32)  — attenuated, marginal
  palbociclib x ER    : +1.58   (p ~ 0)     — robust

----------------------------------------------------------------
OVERALL CONCLUSIONS
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. The PFS signal in this 50k-patient breast-cancer cohort is dominated
   by classical prognostic factors: stage_iv (~ -1.5 mo), ECOG
   (-1.2 mo per point), brain_mets (-1.0 mo), albumin (+0.5 mo per g/dL),
   weight loss (-0.07 mo per pct point), Ki-67 (-0.02 mo per pct), and
   the ER+/HER2+/PIK3CA-mut axis.

2. Of the six therapies, only treatment_palbociclib carries a large
   unconditional positive PFS signal (~+1.1 months), entirely driven by
   the ER+ subgroup. In ER+/PIK3CA-WT patients the benefit is even
   larger; in ER+/PIK3CA-mut patients it is largely abrogated.
   This is the strongest precision-medicine pattern in the dataset.

3. olaparib carries a real but modest PFS benefit confined to BRCA-mutated
   patients; its effect attenuates after adjustment but the sign is
   preserved.

4. trastuzumab x HER2, sacituzumab x HER2-low/TNBC, pembrolizumab x
   MSI-high, and tamoxifen x ER are NOT encoded in this dataset.
   Trastuzumab in HER2+ patients shows a non-significant slightly
   negative trend, suggesting the synthetic data generator did not
   simulate the canonical HER2-targeted benefit.

5. Demographics (race, insurance, rurality, education), comorbidities,
   symptom grades, prior-therapy variables, and SNPs are all null with
   respect to PFS. Either the simulator did not encode them or any
   effects are well below the detectable threshold at n=50k.

6. Net: the cohort behaves like a partially realistic synthetic dataset
   that captured strong main prognostic signals and a subset of
   precision-medicine matches (palbociclib-ER, olaparib-BRCA, plus a
   striking palbociclib-PIK3CA negative modifier within ER+) while
   omitting several others (trastuzumab-HER2 in particular).
