Analysis summary for ds001_depmap

I analyzed 50,000 cell-line CRISPR dependency records with 36 anonymized cell-line features and 10 dependency outcomes. Dependency scores are interpreted on their natural scale, where more negative values indicate stronger dependency. I used subgroup-vs-rest mean differences as signed effects and ran a systematic heterogeneity search across 120 one-predicate feature rules, 67,870 two-predicate rules, and targeted three-predicate refinements for every dependency outcome.

BRAF showed a dominant main-effect signal from feature_009. Lines with feature_009 = 1 had mean dependency_BRAF -0.8047 versus -0.2495 in the rest of the cohort, effect -0.5552, p <= 1e-300. Skin lineage (feature_011 = skin) was also more negative, but the systematic subgroup search did not find a stable modifier that improved materially on feature_009 = 1 as the primary rule.

EGFR dependency was concentrated in feature_022 = 1 lines. The mean dependency_EGFR was -0.6010 in feature_022 = 1 versus -0.2524 in all other lines, effect -0.3486, p <= 1e-300. Smaller subgroups with feature_022 = 1 plus low feature_015 and high feature_023 were even more negative, but feature_022 = 1 remained the cleanest supported driver.

ERBB2 dependency was concentrated in feature_024 = 1 lines. These lines had mean dependency_ERBB2 -0.6856 versus -0.2535 in the rest of the cohort, effect -0.4321, p <= 1e-300. Breast-lineage/high-feature_013/feature_024 = 1 cells were slightly more negative, but the feature_024 = 1 main rule explained most of the signal.

KRAS dependency was concentrated in feature_014 = 1 lines. The mean dependency_KRAS was -0.5594 versus -0.2520, effect -0.3075, p <= 1e-300. Pancreatic and colorectal lineages were enriched for dependency, but adding lineage predicates did not outperform the broad feature_014 = 1 rule. A small feature_014 = 1, high-feature_016, low-feature_028 subgroup was somewhat more negative.

RIT1 dependency was weaker but significant for feature_027 = 1. Lines with feature_027 = 1 had mean dependency_RIT1 -0.3527 versus -0.2572, effect -0.0955, p <= 1e-300. The best lineage refinement was feature_011 = brain with feature_027 = 1, mean -0.4047 versus -0.2650, effect -0.1397, p = 1.22e-13.

KIF18A showed the strongest heterogeneous pattern. Colorectal lines (feature_011 = colorectal) had mean dependency_KIF18A -0.7204 versus -0.2524, effect -0.4680, p <= 1e-300. Adding high feature_028 greatly strengthened the pattern: colorectal lines with feature_028 >= 1.427 had mean -1.0650 versus -0.2677, effect -0.7973, p <= 1e-300. The best complete subgroup I found was feature_011 = colorectal, feature_028 >= 1.427, feature_020 = 0, and feature_035 = 1; this group had mean dependency_KIF18A -1.3952 versus -0.2678 in all others, effect -1.1274, p <= 1e-300. Threshold probing supported a monotone high-feature_028 component.

TMED10 had a smaller dependency association involving high feature_016 and high feature_026. High feature_016 alone had effect -0.0407, p = 1.11e-14. High feature_016 with high feature_026 had effect -0.0893, p = 1.27e-8, and the best three-predicate refinement was only modestly stronger.

DHX9 dependency was associated with high feature_016 and high feature_023. High feature_016 alone had effect -0.0433, high feature_023 alone had effect -0.0314, and the joint high-feature_016/high-feature_023 subgroup had mean -0.4174 versus -0.3327, effect -0.0847, p = 5.09e-8.

POLR2A dependency was most clearly associated with high feature_016. High feature_016 lines had mean dependency_POLR2A -0.5281 versus -0.4070, effect -0.1211, p <= 1e-300. Pair and triple subgroup searches found only modest refinements, so high feature_016 is the most stable rule.

RPL11 dependency was associated with high feature_003, feature_018 = 1, and high feature_016. High feature_003 alone had effect -0.1257, p <= 1e-300; feature_018 = 1 had effect -0.1091. The high-feature_003/high-feature_016 subgroup had mean -0.4193 versus -0.2677, effect -0.1515, p <= 1e-300. A smaller high-feature_003/high-feature_016-p75/high-feature_033 subgroup was more negative but less stable.

Overall, the clearest outcome-specific dependency drivers were feature_009 for BRAF, feature_022 for EGFR, feature_024 for ERBB2, feature_014 for KRAS, feature_027 for RIT1, and the multi-feature colorectal/high-feature_028/feature_020 = 0/feature_035 = 1 subgroup for KIF18A. The housekeeping-like outcomes TMED10, DHX9, POLR2A, and RPL11 showed broader feature_016/feature_003/feature_023-associated patterns with smaller effect sizes than the oncogene and KIF18A subgroup signals.
