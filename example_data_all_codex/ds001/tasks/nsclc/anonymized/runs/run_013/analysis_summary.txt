Dataset ds001_nsclc contained 50,000 patients, 73 opaque features, and the continuous outcome pfs_months. I treated the analysis as exploratory because the feature labels are de-identified. Effects below are signed on the pfs_months scale and p-values are not multiplicity-adjusted.

The strongest main effect was feature_041. In simple OLS, each 1-unit increase in feature_041 was associated with +0.1699 months of PFS (p < 1e-300), and the all-feature adjusted coefficient was nearly identical at +0.1684 months. Feature_036 was also dominant: mean PFS declined from 4.3268 months at feature_036=0 to 3.1854 at feature_036=1 and 2.1519 at feature_036=2. The adjusted feature_036=2 versus 0 coefficient was -2.1775 months (p < 1e-300).

Several binary and categorical main effects were robust. Feature_001=1 was associated with lower PFS both unadjusted (-1.4375 months) and adjusted (-1.4168 months). Feature_042=1 was similarly negative (unadjusted -0.9207, adjusted -0.9122 months). Feature_015=squamous was lower than adenocarcinoma by -0.8569 months unadjusted and -0.7229 months adjusted. Feature_025 former and never categories were favorable relative to current; adjusted coefficients were +0.5517 and +0.5453 months, respectively. Feature_004 was independently negative (-0.0732 months per unit), and feature_056 was independently positive (+0.4630 months per unit). Feature_051 had a statistically detectable but small negative adjusted effect (-0.00045 months per unit).

Some unadjusted findings were screening artifacts. Feature_057=1 looked strongly negative unadjusted (-0.8104 months), but after all-feature adjustment its coefficient was only -0.0283 months and not significant at 0.05 (p=0.0629). Feature_028 and feature_066 also disappeared after adjustment, with adjusted effects of -0.0054 months (p=0.471) and -0.0007 months (p=0.949). This suggests their marginal associations were largely explained by the dominant covariates.

The most clinically meaningful heterogeneity involved feature_010, feature_009, and feature_037. Feature_010=1 was favorable overall (+0.7815 months unadjusted; +0.8924 months adjusted), and feature_009=1 was also favorable (+0.3220 months unadjusted; +0.3231 months adjusted). But their joint effect was much larger than additive: the adjusted feature_010:feature_009 interaction was +2.5406 months (p < 1e-300). Stratified analysis confirmed this: within feature_009=1, feature_010=1 was associated with +2.4372 months, while within feature_009=0 the feature_010 difference was slightly negative (-0.1206 months).

Feature_037 sharply modified those relationships. The adjusted feature_037:feature_010 interaction was -1.6239 months, and feature_037:feature_009 was -0.5843 months. Within feature_037=0, feature_010=1 was associated with +1.5424 months and feature_009=1 with +0.5709 months. Within feature_037=1, feature_010=1 was slightly negative (-0.1517 months) and feature_009=1 was essentially null (+0.0086 months, p=0.771). The three-way interaction feature_037:feature_010:feature_009 was -4.5767 months (p < 1e-300), meaning the large feature_010/feature_009 synergy was concentrated in feature_037=0 patients. The subgroup feature_037=0, feature_010=1, feature_009=1 had mean PFS 7.8760 months versus 3.3188 months in all other patients, a +4.5572 month difference.

Additional interaction checks found smaller heterogeneity. Feature_057 weakened the feature_010 association, with adjusted feature_010:feature_057 interaction -0.8797 months. Feature_036 attenuated the negative effects of feature_001 and feature_042: the feature_036=2 interaction increments were +0.2309 months for feature_001 and +0.1705 months for feature_042. Feature_041 stayed positive at all feature_036 levels, but its slope weakened from +0.1790 months per unit at feature_036=0 to +0.1464 at feature_036=2. Feature_015=squamous slightly attenuated the feature_001 effect (+0.0743 month interaction), but it did not modify the feature_010:feature_009 synergy (interaction +0.0149, p=0.587). Feature_025 also did not strongly modify that synergy; the former category interaction was null and the never category was statistically negative but small relative to the base synergy.

In the all-feature adjusted model, the dominant signals were feature_041, feature_036, feature_001, feature_042, feature_015, feature_010, feature_004, feature_056, feature_025, feature_009, and feature_037. Remaining independent signals were small: feature_029=-0.1095 months, feature_048=-0.0292 months, and feature_023=-0.0192 months. Weak continuous markers such as feature_013 and feature_070 were not significant after full adjustment. Overall, the data support a strong multivariable structure with both large baseline-like main effects and a highly specific feature_010-feature_009-feature_037 interaction pattern in PFS.
