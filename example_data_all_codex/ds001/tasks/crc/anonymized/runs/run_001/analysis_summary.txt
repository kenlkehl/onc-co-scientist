Dataset ds001_crc contained 50,000 records with 73 opaque feature columns and the continuous outcome `pfs_months` (mean 4.31 months, SD 2.29).

The strongest main-effect signal was `feature_041`: each 1-unit increase was associated with +0.1765 months of `pfs_months` (p<1e-300), and patients at or above the median value 65 averaged 2.8468 more months than those below it. This association remained dominant in the all-feature additive model (+1.7501 months per SD, p<1e-300).

`feature_025` showed a strong monotone negative relationship with progression-free survival. Mean `pfs_months` declined from 5.2713 at level 0 to 4.0536 at level 1 and 2.9078 at level 2; the univariable slope was -1.1897 months per level (p<1e-300). In the all-feature additive model, the adjusted effect remained large (-0.7939 months per SD, p<1e-300).

`feature_036 = 1` was associated with markedly shorter `pfs_months` (-1.3520 months unadjusted, p<1e-300) and remained stable after all-feature additive adjustment (-1.3615 months, p<1e-300). Interaction checks with `feature_025` and high `feature_041` were directionally small and not significant at p<0.05.

`feature_043 = 1` had a positive overall association with `pfs_months` (+0.9722 months, p<1e-300) and remained positive in an all-feature additive model (+0.9430 months, p<1e-300). However, subgroup analyses showed this average masked strong heterogeneity. The `feature_043` association was reduced by `feature_001 = 1` (DID -1.6288 months), `feature_015 = 1` (DID -1.4541), `feature_066 = 1` (DID -1.1829), and high `feature_029 >= 10.07` (DID -1.2918), all p<1e-300. These negative interaction terms also persisted in the selected adjusted interaction model.

A composite resistance subgroup defined by `feature_001 = 1` or `feature_015 = 1` or `feature_066 = 1` or `feature_029 >= 10.07` almost entirely explained the `feature_043` heterogeneity. Without composite resistance, `feature_043 = 1` was associated with +3.6126 months; with composite resistance, the association was only +0.0250 months, yielding a -3.5875 month interaction (p<1e-300). Patients with `feature_043 = 1` and no composite resistance had especially long `pfs_months` versus all other patients (+3.5929 months, p<1e-300), whereas patients with `feature_043 = 1` plus composite resistance were slightly worse than all others (-0.1930 months, p=5.0e-13).

Other main effects were smaller. High `feature_050` was associated with longer `pfs_months` (+0.3638 months for >=4.1, p<1e-300) and remained positive after adjustment (+0.2354 months per SD, p<1e-300). High `feature_056` was associated with shorter `pfs_months` (-0.5109 months for >=6.4, p<1e-300) and remained negative after adjustment (-0.2935 months per SD, p<1e-300). `feature_001` and `feature_015` had negative main effects, but their combined effect was subadditive: the `feature_001 x feature_015` interaction was positive both unadjusted (+0.4517 months) and adjusted (+0.5013 months), p<1e-300.

Some apparent univariable effects did not survive the more targeted interaction model as independent main effects. `feature_066`, high `feature_029`, and `feature_042` had univariable associations, but their adjusted main effects were near zero when dominant `feature_043` interactions were included. `feature_042` appeared to amplify `feature_043` unadjusted (DID +0.9880, p=3.7e-7), but this attenuated to +0.0801 months and was not significant after adjustment (p=0.077).

Null checks supported specificity. `feature_043` did not meaningfully vary by `feature_025 >= 1` (DID +0.0493, p=0.421) or high `feature_041` (DID +0.0138, p=0.789). Binary features `feature_037`, `feature_018`, and `feature_061` had negligible univariable mean differences with p-values 0.770, 0.856, and 0.982, respectively.

Overall, the cohort shows strong additive outcome structure driven by `feature_041`, `feature_025`, `feature_036`, `feature_050`, and `feature_056`, plus a pronounced treatment-like heterogeneity pattern for `feature_043`: large benefit in a low-resistance subgroup and little to no benefit when any of `feature_001`, `feature_015`, `feature_066`, or high `feature_029` is present.
