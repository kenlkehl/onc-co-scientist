Analysis summary for ds001_crc

Dataset contained 50000 patients, 73 de-identified features, and outcome pfs_months (mean 4.312, SD 2.293, range 0.000-16.250).
I decoded the local Parquet file and tested 73 univariable main effects with OLS, then screened all 2624 pairwise interaction models of the form pfs_months ~ feature_a + feature_b + feature_a:feature_b. P-values are two-sided normal approximations to large-sample t tests; q-values in transcript.json are Benjamini-Hochberg adjustments within the relevant screen.

Main effects:
- feature_041: effect 0.1765 months per unit, p=<1e-300, BH q=<1e-300.
- feature_025: effect -1.1897 months per unit, p=<1e-300, BH q=<1e-300.
- feature_036: effect -1.3520 months per unit, p=<1e-300, BH q=<1e-300.
- feature_043: effect 0.9722 months per unit, p=<1e-300, BH q=<1e-300.
- feature_056: effect -0.0730 months per unit, p=<1e-300, BH q=<1e-300.
- feature_050: effect 0.4654 months per unit, p=<1e-300, BH q=<1e-300.
- feature_001: effect -0.3268 months per unit, p=<1e-300, BH q=<1e-300.
- feature_015: effect -0.3052 months per unit, p=<1e-300, BH q=<1e-300.
- feature_029: effect -0.0053 months per unit, p=<1e-300, BH q=<1e-300.
- feature_066: effect -0.2279 months per unit, p=3.68e-6, BH q=2.69e-5.
Overall, 14 of 73 main effects were nominally significant and 12 survived BH q<0.05. The clearest positive association was feature_041 (effect 0.1765); the clearest negative association was feature_025 (effect -1.1897).

Adjusted model:
- feature_041: adjusted effect 0.1758 months, p=<1e-300 after including feature_041, feature_025, feature_036, feature_043, feature_056, feature_050, feature_001, feature_015, feature_029, feature_066, feature_042, feature_039.
- feature_025: adjusted effect -1.1715 months, p=<1e-300 after including feature_041, feature_025, feature_036, feature_043, feature_056, feature_050, feature_001, feature_015, feature_029, feature_066, feature_042, feature_039.
- feature_036: adjusted effect -1.3613 months, p=<1e-300 after including feature_041, feature_025, feature_036, feature_043, feature_056, feature_050, feature_001, feature_015, feature_029, feature_066, feature_042, feature_039.
- feature_043: adjusted effect 0.9428 months, p=<1e-300 after including feature_041, feature_025, feature_036, feature_043, feature_056, feature_050, feature_001, feature_015, feature_029, feature_066, feature_042, feature_039.
The leading signals therefore were not just isolated univariable artifacts; the strongest screened features remained significant after mutual adjustment, although effect sizes shifted where features were correlated.

Interactions and subgroup heterogeneity:
- feature_001 x feature_043: interaction effect -1.6288 months, p=<1e-300, BH q=<1e-300.
- feature_015 x feature_043: interaction effect -1.4541 months, p=<1e-300, BH q=<1e-300.
- feature_029 x feature_043: interaction effect -0.0253 months, p=<1e-300, BH q=<1e-300.
- feature_041 x feature_025: interaction effect -0.0072 months, p=<1e-300, BH q=<1e-300.
- feature_064 x feature_060: interaction effect -0.1876 months, p=5.56e-4, BH q=0.133.
- feature_045 x feature_061: interaction effect -0.1888 months, p=9.41e-4, BH q=0.204.
- feature_035 x feature_065: interaction effect 0.0056 months, p=0.00110, BH q=0.206.
Across all pairwise models, 128 interactions were nominally significant and 10 survived BH q<0.05. The strongest interactions often involved feature_001/feature_043, feature_015/feature_043, feature_029/feature_043, feature_041/feature_025, feature_064/feature_060, feature_045/feature_061, feature_035/feature_065, indicating heterogeneous feature-outcome associations rather than purely additive main effects.

Threshold checks:
- Highest quartile of feature_041 (>=71.800): mean pfs_months 6.571 vs 3.550 below threshold; effect 3.0212, p=<1e-300.
- Highest quartile of feature_025 (>=1.000): mean pfs_months 3.791 vs 5.271 below threshold; effect -1.4806, p=<1e-300.
- Highest quartile of feature_056 (>=6.400): mean pfs_months 3.929 vs 4.440 below threshold; effect -0.5109, p=<1e-300.
- Highest quartile of feature_050 (>=4.100): mean pfs_months 4.563 vs 4.199 below threshold; effect 0.3638, p=<1e-300.

Conclusion:
The cohort shows a dominant positive association between feature_041 and pfs_months and strong negative associations for feature_025 and feature_036. Several additional binary and continuous features have smaller but statistically detectable effects. Pairwise interaction testing found statistically strong heterogeneity, especially involving feature_041, feature_025, feature_036, feature_043, feature_056, feature_050, feature_001, and feature_015. Because feature labels are de-identified, the findings should be treated as statistical treatment-biomarker-outcome patterns requiring clinical mapping before interpretation.
