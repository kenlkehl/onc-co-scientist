Analysis summary for ds001_breast

The cohort contained 50000 patients and one continuous outcome, pfs_months (mean 4.693, SD 2.496). I completed the 25-iteration protocol using univariate OLS, all-feature multivariable OLS, pairwise interaction models, subgroup mean comparisons, level-specific contrasts, and a centered quadratic check. P-values use large-sample normal approximations.

Main effects
The strongest univariate associations were: feature_044 +0.176 months (p=<1e-300); feature_027 -1.180 months (p=<1e-300); feature_039 -1.536 months (p=<1e-300); feature_046 +1.098 months (p=<1e-300); feature_015 -1.050 months (p=<1e-300); feature_037 -0.075 months (p=<1e-300); feature_031 -0.559 months (p=<1e-300); feature_069 +0.568 months (p=<1e-300). These hypotheses were statistically supported in the signed directions shown.
In the all-feature model, the leading effects remained directionally consistent for the top signals: feature_044 +0.176 months (p=<1e-300); feature_027 -1.172 months (p=<1e-300); feature_039 -1.549 months (p=<1e-300); feature_046 +1.101 months (p=<1e-300); feature_015 -0.983 months (p=<1e-300). This supports feature_044, feature_027, feature_039, feature_046, and feature_015 as independent outcome-associated features rather than simple proxies for one another.

Interactions and subgroups
The strongest interaction terms were: feature_046:feature_031 -1.623 months (p=<1e-300); feature_046:feature_069 +1.594 months (p=<1e-300); feature_046:feature_062 -0.052 months (p=<1e-300); feature_046:feature_063 -1.364 months (p=<1e-300); feature_046:feature_010 +1.093 months (p=<1e-300). The largest was feature_046:feature_031, indicating that the feature_046 association with pfs_months changed substantially by feature_031 subgroup.
Stratified checks confirmed that pattern: within feature_031=0, feature_046=1 vs 0 differed by 1.659 months, while within feature_031=1 the difference was 0.036 months.

Refinements
Feature_027 showed a dose-like adverse pattern: feature_027=2 vs 0 differed by -2.351 months and feature_027=1 vs 0 differed by -1.196 months, both with p=<1e-300 or smaller.
Feature_044 also had statistically significant curvature after centering (quadratic coefficient 6.04e-4, p=<1e-300), but the linear positive gradient remained the dominant feature_044 pattern on the pfs_months scale.

Overall, this dataset shows large treatment/biomarker-like heterogeneity despite opaque feature labels: higher feature_044 and feature_046 were associated with longer pfs_months, higher feature_027, feature_039, feature_015, and feature_031 were associated with shorter pfs_months, and several interaction terms suggest subgroup-specific outcome differences rather than only additive main effects.
