Dataset ds001_breast contained 50000 records with complete pfs_months values (mean 4.693 months, SD 2.496). I decoded the local Parquet file and treated binary 0/1 features as group indicators; other numeric features were tested per 1-SD increase unless otherwise stated.

Main-effect screening found several strong associations with progression-free survival. The largest positive univariable signals were feature_044 (1.751 months, p=<1e-16), feature_046 (1.098 months, p=<1e-16), feature_069 (0.568 months, p=<1e-16), feature_004 (0.250 months, p=<1e-16), feature_010 (0.362 months, p=<1e-16). The largest negative univariable signals were feature_027 (-0.800 months, p=<1e-16), feature_039 (-1.536 months, p=<1e-16), feature_015 (-1.050 months, p=<1e-16), feature_037 (-0.288 months, p=<1e-16), feature_031 (-0.559 months, p=<1e-16).

A multivariable OLS model including the 12 strongest univariable features (feature_044, feature_027, feature_039, feature_046, feature_015, feature_037, feature_031, feature_069, feature_004, feature_062, feature_063, feature_010) showed that the leading effects were not all interchangeable. Adjusted coefficients for the first four were feature_044: 1.748 months (p=<1e-16); feature_027: -0.794 months (p=<1e-16); feature_039: -1.549 months (p=<1e-16); feature_046: 1.102 months (p=<1e-16).

Subgroup and interaction probing showed non-additive structure. The strongest binary-binary interactions were feature_031 x feature_046: -1.623 months (p=<1e-16); feature_046 x feature_069: 1.594 months (p=<1e-16); feature_046 x feature_063: -1.364 months (p=<1e-16); feature_010 x feature_046: 1.093 months (p=<1e-16); feature_031 x feature_069: -0.800 months (p=<1e-16). For the top interaction, the effect of feature_031 differed sharply by feature_046: feature_046=0: 0.008 months (p=0.7348); feature_046=1: -1.615 months (p=<1e-16).

Binary-by-numeric interaction scans also found heterogeneity: feature_046 x feature_062: -0.637 months per SD interaction (p=<1e-16); feature_031 x feature_062: 0.362 months per SD interaction (p=<1e-16); feature_069 x feature_062: -0.309 months per SD interaction (p=<1e-16); feature_063 x feature_062: 0.256 months per SD interaction (p=<1e-16); feature_010 x feature_062: -0.191 months per SD interaction (p=1.11e-16). Continuous-by-continuous product interactions were weaker but the largest were feature_027 x feature_044: -0.039 months (p=5.55e-8); feature_017 x feature_036: 0.043 months (p=0.0001); feature_006 x feature_008: -0.038 months (p=0.0008).

Null checks were included to avoid only reporting positive findings. Features with negligible univariable evidence included feature_006 (0.001 months, p=0.9358), feature_017 (0.003 months, p=0.8216), feature_029 (-0.013 months, p=0.8192). Overall, the cohort shows a mixture of strong main effects and meaningful subgroup heterogeneity among the opaque feature labels; because the labels are de-identified, the results should be interpreted as treatment/biomarker-like statistical patterns rather than named clinical mechanisms.
