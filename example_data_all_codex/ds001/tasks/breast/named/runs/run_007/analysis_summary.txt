Analysis summary for ds001_breast

I analyzed 50000 breast oncology records with outcome pfs_months (mean 4.693 months, SD 2.496). The main model was adjusted OLS using all original non-outcome columns; continuous predictors were standardized, so continuous coefficients are months per one standard deviation. Interaction tests added one interaction to that full main-effects model unless otherwise noted.

Main effects: disease severity dominated the prognostic structure. stage_iv: -1.549 months for stage IV vs non-stage IV, p=<1e-250 (significant). ecog_ps: -0.794 months per point, p=<1e-250 (significant). has_brain_mets: -0.983 months for present vs absent, p=<1e-250 (significant).
Tumor biology was also strong. er_positive: +0.532 months for positive vs negative, p=<1e-250 (significant). her2_positive: -0.471 months for positive vs negative, p=<1e-250 (significant). pik3ca_mutation: -0.582 months for mutated vs wild-type, p=<1e-250 (significant). ki67_pct: -0.230 months per SD (12.2 points), p=<1e-250 (significant).
Nutritional/systemic burden markers were directionally coherent. albumin_g_dl: +0.238 months per SD (0.50 g/dL), p=<1e-250 (significant). weight_loss_pct_6mo: -0.297 months per SD, p=<1e-250 (significant).
Age had an unexpectedly favorable adjusted association: age_years: +1.749 months per SD (10.0 years), p=<1e-250 (significant).

Treatment-biomarker findings: palbociclib was the dominant treatment signal. treatment_palbociclib main effect: +1.101 months for treated vs untreated, p=<1e-250 (significant). Its association was larger in ER-positive disease and smaller with PIK3CA mutation, higher Ki-67, and triple-negative subtype: palbociclib x er_positive: +1.559 months interaction, p=<1e-250 (significant). palbociclib x pik3ca_mutation: -1.665 months interaction, p=<1e-250 (significant). palbociclib x ki67_pct: -0.646 months interaction per SD, p=<1e-250 (significant). palbociclib x triple-negative: -1.337 months interaction, p=<1e-250 (significant).
Other clinically plausible therapy-biomarker hypotheses were weak or refuted: tamoxifen x ER-positive, trastuzumab x HER2-positive, olaparib x any BRCA mutation, and pembrolizumab x triple-negative status did not show positive significant support. Sacituzumab govitecan showed a modest positive interaction with brain metastases: sacituzumab x has_brain_mets: +0.239 months interaction, p=2.66e-5 (significant).

Aggregate and subtype analyses: metastatic_burden_count: -0.119 months per additional metastatic/effusion site, p=<1e-250 (significant). comorbidity_count: -0.004 months per additional comorbidity, p=0.2991 (not significant). triple-negative subtype: -0.491 months for triple-negative vs other, p=<1e-250 (significant).
Overall, the supported patterns were disease extent/performance status, ER-favorable and HER2/PIK3CA/Ki-67-adverse biology, nutrition/cachexia markers, and a highly specific palbociclib-biomarker interaction profile. Several expected treatment-biomarker relationships outside palbociclib were not supported in these adjusted analyses.
