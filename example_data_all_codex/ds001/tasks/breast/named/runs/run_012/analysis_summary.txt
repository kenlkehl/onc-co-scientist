Dataset ds001_breast contains 50,000 breast oncology records with no missing values and one outcome, pfs_months. Mean PFS was 4.693 months (SD 2.496; range 0 to 16.660). I used adjusted OLS models on the natural month scale, then added product terms for targeted subgroup and interaction tests. Very small p-values are reported as 1e-300 when they underflowed the normal approximation used for the large-sample OLS z tests.

The strongest adjusted prognostic patterns were consistent across iterations. Higher age_years was associated with longer PFS (+0.1757 months/year, p<1e-300), while worse ecog_ps (-1.1717 months per point), stage_iv (-1.5487 months), and has_brain_mets (-0.9831 months) were strongly associated with shorter PFS. ER-positive disease had longer PFS (+0.5315 months), whereas HER2-positive disease (-0.4710 months), pik3ca_mutation (-0.5816 months), higher ki67_pct (-0.0189 months per percentage point), lower albumin_g_dl (+0.4757 months per g/dL), higher weight_loss_pct_6mo (-0.0768 months per percentage point), and higher ldh_u_l (-0.0005 months per U/L) all pointed toward worse outcomes.

Treatment main effects were concentrated in palbociclib. treatment_palbociclib was associated with +1.1014 adjusted months of PFS (p<1e-300). treatment_tamoxifen, treatment_trastuzumab, treatment_olaparib, and treatment_pembrolizumab did not show meaningful adjusted main effects. treatment_sacituzumab_govitecan was borderline significant but extremely small (+0.0334 months, p=0.0495), so I would not interpret it as a clinically meaningful main effect.

The main treatment-biomarker finding was palbociclib heterogeneity. The palbociclib association was much larger in er_positive patients (interaction +1.5585 months, p<1e-300); raw palbociclib differences were +1.577 months in ER-positive patients and -0.017 months in ER-negative patients. In contrast, pik3ca_mutation nearly abolished the palbociclib-associated gain (interaction -1.6653 months, p<1e-300); raw differences were +1.659 months in PIK3CA-wildtype patients and +0.036 months in mutated patients. In a simultaneous interaction model, the palbociclib by er_positive interaction remained large (+1.5099 months), the palbociclib by pik3ca_mutation interaction remained strongly negative (-1.6565 months), and pr_positive added only a small additional increment (+0.0575 months, p=0.0296).

PIK3CA also showed biomarker-biomarker heterogeneity: the adverse pik3ca_mutation association was substantially stronger in er_positive patients (interaction -0.8260 months, p<1e-300; -0.8392 months in the simultaneous palbociclib interaction model). This suggests the poor-prognosis PIK3CA signal is concentrated in the ER-positive context in this cohort.

Several clinically plausible treatment-subgroup hypotheses were not supported. The treatment_trastuzumab by her2_positive interaction was null (+0.0219 months, p=0.501), and interactions with her2_amplification and her2_low were also null. Olaparib did not show significant adjusted interaction with brca1_mutation (+0.0774 months, p=0.484) or brca2_mutation (-0.0448 months, p=0.687), despite a small unadjusted BRCA-mutated subgroup difference. Pembrolizumab did not show a significant triple-negative interaction (+0.0326 months, p=0.349), and sacituzumab govitecan did not show a triple-negative interaction (+0.0473 months, p=0.251). Pembrolizumab had a borderline small negative interaction with high nlr (nlr >= 4: -0.0607 months, p=0.0490), which is statistically fragile and much smaller than the palbociclib interactions.

Additional interaction probes did not show meaningful compounding of baseline risk: stage_iv did not interact with has_brain_mets (+0.0074 months, p=0.841), liver_mets (tested separately, p=0.153), or low albumin (-0.0214 months, p=0.410). A small independent brca1_mutation association was detected (-0.0689 months, p=0.0327), but its magnitude was clinically minor.

Overall, the cohort shows a strong prognostic structure dominated by performance status, stage, brain metastases, ER/HER2/PIK3CA biology, proliferation, nutrition/inflammation proxies, and one major treatment-biomarker-treatment interaction: palbociclib benefit appears concentrated in ER-positive and PIK3CA-wildtype disease. Most other treatment-biomarker hypotheses were statistically unsupported after adjustment.
