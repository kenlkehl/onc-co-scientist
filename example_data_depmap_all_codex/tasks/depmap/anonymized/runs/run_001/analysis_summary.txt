Analysis summary for ds001_depmap

I analyzed 50,000 cell-line records with 36 anonymized cell-line features and 10 CRISPR dependency outcomes. More negative dependency scores were treated as stronger knockout dependency. I used Welch mean comparisons for binary, categorical, and threshold-defined subgroups, linear slopes for continuous feature screens, and feature-by-feature interaction and two/three-predicate subgroup searches for dependency heterogeneity. Upper-quartile thresholds used in final subgroup tests were feature_016>=0.964, feature_028>=0.819, feature_023>=5.669, feature_002>=4.439, and feature_031>=0.654; lower-quartile thresholds were feature_017<=1.956 and feature_023<=4.230.

The first iteration tested obvious focal dependency hypotheses. The strongest main-effect results were feature_009=1 for dependency_BRAF (mean -0.8047 versus -0.2495, effect -0.5552, p<=1e-300), feature_024=1 for dependency_ERBB2 (effect -0.4321, p<=1e-300), feature_022=1 for dependency_EGFR (effect -0.3486, p<=1e-300), feature_014=1 for dependency_KRAS (effect -0.3075, p<=1e-300), and feature_027=1 for dependency_RIT1 (effect -0.0955, p<=1e-300). These hypotheses were supported.

A continuous feature screen showed that higher feature_016 was associated with more negative scores for all ten dependency outcomes. The slope was largest for dependency_POLR2A (-0.3048 per 1.0 feature_016 increase, p<=1e-300), with smaller but significant negative slopes for dependency_BRAF, dependency_EGFR, dependency_ERBB2, dependency_KRAS, dependency_RIT1, dependency_KIF18A, dependency_TMED10, dependency_DHX9, and dependency_RPL11. This looked like a broad dependency-modifying or screen-state axis rather than a single-gene-specific signal.

The most striking heterogeneity was for dependency_KIF18A. Main-effect tests showed strong negative associations for feature_011=colorectal (effect -0.4680, p<=1e-300), feature_035=1 (effect -0.2921, p<=1e-300), and feature_028>=0.819 (effect -0.2168, p<=1e-300). Pairwise interaction tests showed that these were not merely additive: colorectal by feature_035=1 had interaction effect -0.6822, colorectal by high feature_028 had interaction effect -0.6691, and feature_035=1 by high feature_028 had interaction effect -0.4698, all p<=1e-300.

The refined KIF18A subgroup feature_011=colorectal AND feature_035=1 AND feature_028>=0.819 was the best-supported concentrated dependency pattern I found. Its mean dependency_KIF18A score was -1.1119 in 3,133 cell lines versus -0.2530 in all others, effect -0.8589 with p<=1e-300. This subgroup definition is substantially sharper than any KIF18A single-feature main effect.

For EGFR, adding high feature_016 to feature_022=1 produced a slightly more concentrated subgroup: feature_022=1 AND feature_016>=0.964 had mean dependency_EGFR -0.6253 versus -0.2727 otherwise, effect -0.3526, p<=1e-300. For RIT1, feature_027=1 AND feature_023<=4.230 had mean dependency_RIT1 -0.3761 versus -0.2633, effect -0.1128, p<=1e-300. ERBB2 and KRAS were best summarized by their main binary features, feature_024=1 and feature_014=1 respectively; extra predicates did not change the interpretation as much as they did for KIF18A.

For the remaining dependency outcomes, the systematic heterogeneity screen found smaller exploratory subgroups. dependency_TMED10 was most negative in feature_016>=0.964 AND feature_027=1 AND feature_017<=1.956 (effect -0.1022, p=3.25e-8). dependency_DHX9 was more negative in feature_016>=0.964 AND feature_023>=5.669 (effect -0.0595, p<=1e-300). dependency_POLR2A was more negative in feature_016>=0.964 AND feature_010=adenocarcinoma AND feature_002>=4.439 (effect -0.1272, p=6.38e-12). dependency_RPL11 had component signals for feature_018=1 and high feature_003, and the strongest refined subgroup was feature_016>=0.964 AND feature_018=1 AND feature_031>=0.654 (effect -0.1512, p<=1e-300). These were supported statistically but are weaker and more exploratory than the KIF18A interaction.

Overall conclusions: the dataset contains several strong main-effect dependency relationships, especially BRAF-feature_009, ERBB2-feature_024, EGFR-feature_022, KRAS-feature_014, and RIT1-feature_027. The clearest multivariable biological pattern is dependency_KIF18A concentrated in colorectal cell lines with feature_035=1 and high feature_028. High feature_016 is a broad modifier associated with stronger dependencies across many outcomes, most prominently dependency_POLR2A, but it does not explain away the KIF18A-specific three-feature interaction.
