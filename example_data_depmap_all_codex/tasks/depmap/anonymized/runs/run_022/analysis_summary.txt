CRISPR dependency map ds001_depmap analysis summary

I analyzed 50,000 cell-line records with 36 anonymized features and 10 dependency outcomes. More negative dependency scores indicate stronger dependency. I used Welch subgroup tests for binary/categorical rules, single-feature linear regressions for continuous features after standardization, four-cell OLS interaction contrasts for pairwise heterogeneity, and an explicit systematic screen over 120 categorical, binary, and continuous-tail predicates. FDR correction was applied within the pairwise interaction screen.

The strongest main-effect dependency signals were clear and highly significant. `feature_009 == 1` defined a BRAF-dependent group (mean -0.8047 versus -0.2495; effect -0.5552, p underflowed to 0). `feature_022 == 1` similarly marked EGFR dependency (effect -0.3486, p=0), `feature_024 == 1` marked ERBB2 dependency (effect -0.4321, p=0), and `feature_014 == 1` marked KRAS dependency (effect -0.3075, p=0). Lineage/subtype checks such as skin-by-`feature_009`, brain-by-`feature_022`, HER2-enriched-by-`feature_024`, and pancreatic-by-`feature_014` did not survive FDR as robust modifiers; their subgroup means were often negative because the driver feature itself was enriched.

`dependency_RIT1` had a smaller but convincing main effect: `feature_027 == 1` shifted the mean by -0.0955 (p=1.55e-68). A brain-by-`feature_027` subgroup was more negative (subgroup effect -0.1397), but its interaction did not survive the systematic screen.

`dependency_KIF18A` showed the dominant heterogeneity pattern in the dataset. Colorectal lines were more dependent (effect -0.4680), as were `feature_035 == 1` lines (effect -0.2921) and high-`feature_028` lines (effect -0.2168). The interaction screen showed that these were not merely additive: colorectal-by-`feature_035` had interaction -0.6822, colorectal-by-high-`feature_028` had interaction -0.6691, and `feature_035`-by-high-`feature_028` had interaction -0.4698, all with FDR q=0. `feature_020 == 1` suppressed this dependency: colorectal/`feature_020 == 1` had interaction +0.5714 and mean -0.2501, close to background. The complete sensitive rule was `feature_011 == colorectal` AND `feature_035 == 1` AND `feature_028 >= 0.819` AND `feature_020 == 0`; this group had mean -1.2990 versus -0.2530 in all other lines (effect -1.0460, p=0). The same rule with `feature_020 == 1` lost the dependency (mean -0.2557; effect +0.0517 versus the rest).

For broad or core-like dependencies, `feature_016` was repeatedly associated with more negative scores: TMED10 slope -0.0203 per SD, DHX9 slope -0.0218 per SD, and POLR2A slope -0.0612 per SD. POLR2A was best summarized by high `feature_016`: `feature_016 >= 0.964` had mean -0.4972 versus -0.3928 otherwise (effect -0.1043), and no POLR2A pairwise modifier survived FDR.

The systematic heterogeneity screen found smaller robust signals outside KIF18A. For TMED10, `feature_009 == 0` AND `feature_016 >= 1.088` had interaction -0.0641 (p=9.91e-05, q=0.0264) and subgroup effect -0.0474; `feature_012 == 1` AND `feature_017 >= 4.292` also had interaction -0.0638 (q=0.0421). For DHX9, `feature_011 == ovarian` AND `feature_002 <= 2.289` had interaction -0.0721 (q=0.0402) and subgroup effect -0.0683. For RPL11, the clearest dependency-enriched effects were additive: `feature_018 == 1` effect -0.1091 and standardized `feature_003` slope -0.0544. A combined high-risk subgroup `feature_018 == 1` AND `feature_003 >= 0.428` AND `feature_016 >= 0.964` had effect -0.1333. The robust RPL11 interaction found by the screen was suppressive, not dependency-enabling: `feature_003 <= -0.412` AND `feature_023 >= 6.33` had interaction +0.0793 (q=0.0407) and was less negative than the rest.

Final best-supported subgroup calls: BRAF, `feature_009 == 1`; EGFR, `feature_022 == 1`; ERBB2, `feature_024 == 1`; KRAS, `feature_014 == 1`; RIT1, `feature_027 == 1`; KIF18A, `feature_011 == colorectal` AND `feature_035 == 1` AND `feature_028 >= 0.819` AND `feature_020 == 0`; TMED10, `feature_009 == 0` AND `feature_016 >= 1.088`; DHX9, `feature_011 == ovarian` AND `feature_002 <= 2.289`; POLR2A, `feature_016 >= 0.964`; RPL11, `feature_018 == 1` AND `feature_003 >= 0.428` AND `feature_016 >= 0.964`, with an additional suppressive interaction for low `feature_003` and high `feature_023`.
