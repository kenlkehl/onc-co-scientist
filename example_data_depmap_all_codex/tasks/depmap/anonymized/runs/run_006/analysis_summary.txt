Rows: 50000; features: 36; dependency outcomes: 10. More negative dependency scores indicate stronger dependency.

Iteration 1: broad main-effect screen
The largest univariate signals for most dependency outcomes were modest. The strongest overall single-feature signal was for dependency_KIF18A in feature_011="colorectal" lines, with feature_035=1 also showing a strong negative shift.

Iteration 2: systematic pairwise dependency-heterogeneity search
Every dependency outcome was screened over feature-by-feature pairwise subgroup definitions. dependency_KIF18A stood out: feature_011="colorectal" combined with feature_035=1 produced a large negative subgroup effect and a large negative interaction contrast. The same systematic search found only small, weak pairwise subgroup effects for the other outcomes.

Iteration 3: focused KIF18A stratified refinement
Within feature_011="colorectal", feature_035=1 lines had much stronger KIF18A dependency than feature_035=0 colorectal lines (effect -0.6834, p=0), supporting an interaction rather than a simple lineage-only effect.

Iteration 4: complete KIF18A subgroup definition
Adding feature_020=0 sharpened the KIF18A subgroup. The complete subgroup effect versus all other lines was -0.8398 (p=0), and the within-colorectal/feature_035=1 comparison showed that feature_020=1 suppresses the dependency signal.

Iteration 5: cross-outcome specificity checks
Several non-KIF18A outcomes had clear main effects, especially dependency_BRAF with feature_009=1, dependency_EGFR with feature_022=1, dependency_ERBB2 with feature_024=1, and dependency_KRAS with feature_014=1. Their strongest pairwise heterogeneity effects were much smaller than the complete KIF18A subgroup effect.

Iteration 6: three-feature subgroup search
A three-feature check over the strongest KIF18A modifiers recovered feature_011="colorectal", feature_035=1, and feature_020=0 as the strongest interpretable rule.

Iteration 7: multivariable confirmation
The colorectal/feature_035=1/feature_020=0 indicator remained strongly negative after adjusting for the component main effects (coefficient -0.8326, p=0).

Iteration 8: robustness to near-neighbor comparison
The complete KIF18A subgroup was still more negative than related two-predicate lines that did not meet the full rule (effect -0.8381, p=0).

Iteration 9: outcome specificity of the complete subgroup
The complete colorectal/feature_035=1/feature_020=0 subgroup was specific for dependency_KIF18A. No other dependency outcome showed a comparably large shift under the same subgroup rule.

Iteration 10: final synthesis
The best-supported biological pattern in this anonymized DepMap-style screen is a KIF18A dependency concentrated in feature_011="colorectal", feature_035=1, feature_020=0 cell lines. The effect is large on the dependency score scale (-0.8398 versus all other lines), statistically decisive, robust to near-neighbor and multivariable checks, and not mirrored across the other dependency outcomes.
